Plant Disease Detection System using DNN and IoT
[ Android, IoT, CNN ]

The occurrence of leaf diseases in ridge gourd plants can significantly impact crop productivity, leading to losses for farmers. Leaf diseases are primarily caused by bacteria, fungi, viruses, and other pathogens. To effectively control these diseases, accurate diagnosis of plant diseases is essential. This study focuses on leveraging Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) technology to aid in the identification and classification of ridge gourd leaf diseases. Additionally, IoT (Internet of Things) devices are utilized to gather supporting data such as temperature, humidity, and other relevant environmental factors.
